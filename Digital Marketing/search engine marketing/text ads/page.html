1-Highlight what makes you unique
2-Inculde numbers
3-Use a call-to-action
4-Create a mobile optimized experience
5-Match your ad to your keywords
6-Match your ad to your landing page
7-Create at least two text ads per ad group
8-Do competitive research
9-Avoid the most common mistakes
