Social Ad Basics: Optimization



How pricing on social media platforms works??

To reach a cetain audience you are participating in an auction, when you run an ad, you bidding against other advertisers who try to reach the same people



-->Since finding the right bid can be quite tricky, most platforms offer to do the bidding for you, if you want to find the bid yourself the app platform often gives you some guidance with a suggested bid

The two most common pricing options:
1-Paying for impressions (Cost per mille CPM)

2-Paying for link clicks (Cost per click CPC)

Cost Per Click
 vs 
Cost Per Impression
Cost per click
 is a method in which advertisers 
pay
 only if their ad is clicked on by a user, but there is no 
cost
 to display the ad
 itself. 
Cost per
 (thousand) 
impression
 is a model that charges advertisers each time an ad is shown, regardless of whether or not it is clicked.
  

We choose CPM or CPC based on:

1-Campaign objectives
2-Total budget
3-Risk tolerance



CTR-->click through rate

ROI(Return On Investment)-->The analysis that tells you if the money you spent to acquire a customer results in a value to the business
