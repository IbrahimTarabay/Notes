Tips: Writing Ad Copy

1-Be Concise
2-Tell them why they should care
3-Have a clear call to action
4-Create a sense of urgency if applicable
5-Tailor ad copy and language to your audience-->if different audiences value different aspects of your product, create separate ads
6-Align the ad copy with creatives and landing page
7-Include your brand if your audience will recognize it
8-Test ad copies with separate ads

Ad Creatives&Copy

What makes a great image for your Facebook ad? What are criteria that can help you choose the right ad creatives?
 

1-Capture your audience’s attention
2-Reflect your brand’s personality
3-Tailor ad creatives to your target audience
4-Show product in its typical environment
5-If you pick stock images, make sure they represent your brand and target your audience
6-Use high-resolution images and make sure all important elements are visible
7-Make sure your images look good on mobile
8-Little to no text overlay
9-Test images with separate ads
