#ifconfig (it shows you your different interface types and the IP address associated with them)

#iwconfig (wireless penetration testing)

#arp -a (is going to show you the IP address it talks to and the MAC address associated)

#netstat -ano (it shows you active connection running on your machine)

#route (it tells you where your traffic exits essentially)

route note: when it goes out gateway it's doing NAT (network address translation) and it's running off your computer, it's important to know route because there could be a machine that you're attacking that has multiple route.

pivoting: when you switch a network from one to other but you're using a machine
