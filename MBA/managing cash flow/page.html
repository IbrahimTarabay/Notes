How can we measure how long it takes for assets and liabilities to turn into cash?

Ans: Cash conversion period ratios

ex:
if we have 5000$ of inventory and the average daily COGS(cost of goods sold) is 100$ then we have 50 days of COGS in inventory

another way to think about it is if we put another 1$ into inventory it won't be converted into cash(or COGS), inventory issues can destroy a company

Inventory to sale conversion period = average inventories/COGS/365

if we sell our product on credit can we track how long it takes us to collect cash?

Sale to cash conversion period = 
average receivables/sales/365

Purchase to payment conversion period =
average payables + average accrued liabilities/COGS/365
