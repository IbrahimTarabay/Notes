How to start a Successful company

TAM-->total addressable market

-->venture capitalists don't invest in companies less than TAM or the market size at least 20$ billion, because we want our underlying companies we invest in to get at least 5% market share longer term, 5% of 20$ billion is a billion dollars in revenue annually, so you want to invest in companies or start companies that have a massive total addressable market (TAM)

google: your industry + TAM

or pick the biggest 30 companies in your industry and add up the revenue from each of those 30 companies and that is close to TAM

Note: you don't have to be a rocket scientist to start an internet company

Trends examples:
1-cloud(cost of set up company is very small, once you set it up you have access to billions of consumers on line)

2-Construction or engineering market in the middle east

3-security companies(there's going to be an incredible need for more private companies creating security products)

4-IOT(that's a theme that has legs which will work for a long time for an investment)

5-Hollywood(fiction in hollywood becomes reality most of the time, example: her movie, i think the next big operating system that matters will be voice based, so today apple is embracing this with siri which is getting better)
