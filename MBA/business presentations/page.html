Important Presentation Rules:

1-Less is more
2-Keep it simple
3-The medium is often more important than the message(How you present is often more important than what you present)
4-inspire, Be passionate
5-Deliver an experience instead of presentation
6-tell story
7- 3 items on each works well
8- 3 background layers in photos
9-use metaphor
10-customer testimonials work
11-short video clips work
12-have high energy (if you have no energy you have nothing)
13-us vs them creates loyalty (introduce antagonist, who is the enemy? and after bad guy introduced then show hero)
14-Humor + self "deprecation" work(making fun of yourself works very well)
15-make eye contact with many
16-Continuous presentations < 10 minutes (break with videos)
17-Repeat you important message
18-Use your hands
19-Use dramatic pauses(you want to use dramatic pauses every while and then stress an important or controversial topic)

summary:
present with passion, right from the heart, don't read a script
