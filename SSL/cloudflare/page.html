Cloudflare-->is a worldwide cloud reverse proxy with caching(serve content much faster), DDoS mitigation, it allows you to hide ip address of your real webservers, save bandwidth of your real webservers





-->caching of the web pages on Reverse Proxy will save resources on web server





-->All of that is offered in free plan

-->CloudFlare automatically apply free TLS certification(multidomain certificate) to your website.
---------------------------------------------------------
-->free certificate was signed by intermediate certification authority



----------------------------------------------------------
-->The certificates auto-renewed by cloudflare each year



-->it's common name is not equal to your domain name but we can use it on our domain

-->if you want to get dedicated certificate where a common name will be equal to your domain name, you can purchase from cloudflare

We have two Certificates:

1-Edge Certificate(automatically generated) on cloudflare

2-Origin Certificate(are only valid for encryption between Cloudflare and your origin server) on your server





-->you can use self signed certificate between your server and cloudflare(it's not trusted by any browser)
